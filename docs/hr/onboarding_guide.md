# Onboarding Guide

This document provides a complete overview of the onboarding process for new employees. HR and managers must follow these steps to ensure a smooth transition.

## 1. Pre‑Start Preparation
HR must prepare the employment contract, create system accounts, and schedule orientation. Managers must assign a mentor and prepare the employee’s workspace.

## 2. First Day
New employees attend orientation, receive equipment, and review company policies. They meet their team and complete required training modules.

## 3. First Week
Employees must complete security training, set up development tools (if applicable), and meet with their manager to review responsibilities. HR must verify that all paperwork is completed.

## 4. First Month
Employees should begin contributing to team tasks. Managers must provide feedback and ensure the employee understands expectations. HR conducts a 30‑day check‑in.

## 5. Probation Period
The probation period lasts 3 months. Managers evaluate performance and provide guidance. HR documents the final evaluation.

This guide ensures consistent onboarding across all departments.
