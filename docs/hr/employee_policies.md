# Employee Policies

This document outlines the company’s core HR policies. All employees must follow these rules to maintain a safe, respectful, and productive workplace.

## 1. Code of Conduct
Employees must treat colleagues with respect, avoid harassment, and maintain professional behavior. Discrimination of any kind is prohibited. Violations may result in disciplinary action.

## 2. Working Hours
Standard working hours are 9:00–17:00. Flexible schedules may be approved by managers. Employees must track their hours accurately.

## 3. Remote Work
Remote work is allowed for eligible roles. Employees must maintain reliable internet access and follow security guidelines when working remotely.

## 4. Time Off
Employees receive annual leave, sick leave, and personal days. All time off must be requested through the HR portal. Unplanned absences must be reported to managers immediately.

## 5. Confidentiality
Employees must protect company data and avoid sharing sensitive information. Access to confidential documents is restricted based on role.

These policies ensure fairness, safety, and compliance across the company.
